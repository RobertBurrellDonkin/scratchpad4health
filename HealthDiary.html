Check For Meningitis

Young people (like you) usually fight off flu quickly without needing to bother with appointments. Meningitis is rare and responds well to treatment but sometimes the symptoms feel a little like the flu. Read more about Meningitis on NHS Choices.

Some early warning signs are:

  • pain in arms, legs, knees or elbows
  • unusually cold hands and feet, or shivering
  • pale or blotchy skin and blue lips

Do you feel any of these?
